# 🐢 TurtleMod Team

We are the official development group behind **TurtleMod**, working to support users, improve the platform, and grow the entire TurtleMod ecosystem.

## 💡 What We Do
- Help users who are learning or building with TurtleMod  
- Develop new features, tools, and improvements  
- Maintain the TurtleMod Editor, Engine, and extensions  
- Create documentation, examples, and learning resources  
- Support the community with guidance and updates  

## 🌱 Our Mission
To make coding fun, creative, and accessible for everyone — especially beginners and young learners — through a modular, visual, and friendly environment.

## 🧩 Projects We Maintain
- **TurtleMod Editor**  
- **TurtleMod Engine**  
- **TurtleMod Extensions**  
- **TurtleMod Docs**  
- More coming soon

## 🤝 Join the Community
We welcome contributors, testers, educators, and curious learners.  
Whether you're fixing a bug, suggesting an idea, or building something new — you're part of the TurtleMod world.
