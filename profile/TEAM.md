# 🐢 TurtleMod Team Structure

Welcome to the official TurtleMod Team organization.  
This document explains every team, what they do, and how they help keep TurtleMod friendly, safe, and growing.

---

## 🌐 Website Developers
**Team name:** `website-developers`  
**Role:** Maintains and improves the TurtleMod website.  
**What they do:**
- Keep the website friendly and welcoming  
- Update pages and fix layout issues  
- Maintain the TurtleMod IDE page  
- Ensure accessibility and good user experience  

---

## 🧩 Extension Viewers
**Team name:** `extension-viewers`  
**Role:** Reviews and tests TurtleMod extensions.  
**What they do:**
- Check extensions for safety and quality  
- Test new features before release  
- Give feedback to extension creators  
- Help maintain a safe extension ecosystem  

---

## 💬 TurtleMod Chatters
**Team name:** `turtlemod-chatters`  
**Role:** Friendly community helpers.  
**What they do:**
- Chat with users  
- Answer questions  
- Help newcomers understand TurtleMod  
- Keep the community positive and kind  

---

## 🛠️ Core Developers *(future team)*
**Team name:** `core-developers`  
**Role:** Works on the main TurtleMod engine.  
**What they do:**
- Fix bugs  
- Add new core features  
- Maintain the main codebase  

---

## 📦 Module Creators *(future team)*
**Team name:** `module-creators`  
**Role:** Builds official TurtleMod modules.  
**What they do:**
- Create new block categories  
- Add new features through modules  
- Maintain module compatibility  

---

## 📝 Documentation Writers *(future team)*
**Team name:** `documentation-writers`  
**Role:** Writes and updates TurtleMod documentation.  
**What they do:**
- Create guides and tutorials  
- Maintain the docs website  
- Help users understand features  

---

## 🧪 Testers *(future team)*
**Team name:** `testers`  
**Role:** Tests new features, modules, and extensions.  
**What they do:**
- Find bugs  
- Report issues  
- Help improve stability  

---

## 🎨 Designers *(future team)*
**Team name:** `designers`  
**Role:** Works on visuals and UI.  
**What they do:**
- Create icons, themes, and UI layouts  
- Improve the look and feel of TurtleMod  

---

# 🤝 How to Join a Team

Anyone who wants to help TurtleMod grow is welcome to join a team.  
Joining is simple:

### **1. Open an Issue**
Create a new issue in this repository with the title:
Team Join Request: <team-name>

Example:
Team Join Request: website-developers

### **2. Tell us who you are**
Include:
- Your GitHub username  
- The team you want to join  
- Why you want to join  
- Any skills or experience (optional)

### **3. Wait for approval**
A TurtleMod owner or moderator will review your request.  
If approved, you’ll be added to the team.

### **4. Start contributing**
Once added, you can:
- Access the team’s repos  
- Join discussions  
- Help with tasks  
- Work with other team members
